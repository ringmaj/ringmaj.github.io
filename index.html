LIDAR Module

Single point short-range LIDAR. IP65 Enclosure, waterproof for rainy days. Detectable range of 0.1-12 meters at 1000Hz and 3.6° FOV. Communication handled via UART/I2C.

Camera Module

170° HD Wide angle lens to provide maximum coverage and give a clear image of surroundings. Interfaces via RCA output. IP69 rating reliably waterproof for rain/washdowns.

Display Module

4 different camera angles to provide maximum coverage and give you a clear image of your surroundings.

ESP32 Module

ESP32-WROOM-32 chip. Low-power device operating at 3.3V with 240Mhz CPU and 4MB FLASH, 28 GPIO pins. Interfaces with composite video libraries and manages camera/LIDAR outputs to send information to the display module.
